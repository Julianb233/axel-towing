## Why BOMA matters for Axle

Commercial buildings (office, retail, mixed-use) have different tow requirements than apartments — lower-volume, higher-value contracts. BOMA members manage Class-A buildings downtown and in the Camelback corridor where Axle currently has thin coverage. Persona research identified 8 BOMA-affiliated decision-makers as priority outreach (see `docs/research/2026-05-07-market-persona-press.md`).
