## Approved Q&A

### 1. 24/7 Dispatch

Q: Is Axle Towing available 24/7?

A: Yes. Towing dispatch is available 24/7. Vehicle release and office visits are handled during posted office hours or by arrangement. Call 480-288-5526 for current release instructions.

### 2. Vehicle Release

Q: Can I pick up my vehicle any time of day?

A: Vehicle release is not handled as a 24/7 walk-in office service. Call 480-288-5526 before coming in so our team can confirm your vehicle status, release instructions, required documents, and current office availability.

### 3. Abandoned Vehicle Removal

Q: Does Axle Towing remove abandoned vehicles from private property?

A: Yes. Axle Towing helps apartment communities, HOA boards, commercial properties, and property managers remove abandoned vehicles from private property. Call 480-288-5526 to request a property assessment.

### 4. Private Property Towing

Q: Does Axle Towing handle private property towing?

A: Yes. Axle Towing provides private property towing and parking enforcement for apartment complexes, HOA communities, retail centers, offices, and commercial properties across the Phoenix metro area.

### 5. HOA Parking Enforcement

Q: Can Axle Towing help an HOA enforce parking rules?

A: Yes. We work with HOA boards and community managers to support parking enforcement, signage, patrols, unauthorized vehicle removal, and reporting.

### 6. Apartment Parking Enforcement

Q: Do you tow from apartment complexes?

A: Yes. Axle Towing supports apartment communities with unauthorized parking removal, abandoned vehicle removal, reserved-space enforcement, guest parking issues, and property-manager towing requests.

### 7. Commercial Property Towing

Q: Do you work with retail centers and commercial properties?

A: Yes. Axle Towing supports retail centers, office buildings, industrial lots, churches, medical facilities, and other commercial properties that need unauthorized vehicles removed from private parking areas.

### 8. Property Owner Cost

Q: How much does parking enforcement cost for property owners?

A: Many private-property parking enforcement programs can be set up at no direct cost to the property owner. Call 480-288-5526 so Axle Towing can review your property and explain the right setup.

### 9. Service Area

Q: What areas does Axle Towing serve?

A: Axle Towing serves Phoenix, Apache Junction, Mesa, Tempe, Scottsdale, Chandler, Gilbert, Glendale, Queen Creek, Gold Canyon, and surrounding Phoenix metro communities.

### 10. Locate A Vehicle

Q: How do I find out if Axle Towing has my vehicle?

A: Call 480-288-5526 or visit axletowing.com/locate-vehicle. Have your license plate, vehicle make/model, and tow location ready so the team can help faster.
