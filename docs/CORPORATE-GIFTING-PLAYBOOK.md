## 1. Strategy Overview

### The "Gift Before the Pitch" Framework

Cold outreach to HOA boards and property managers is noisy. Everyone is emailing and calling them. A physical, branded gift that arrives at their desk is:

1. **Tangible** — It sits there every day. A mug is used 1–3x daily.
2. **Reciprocal** — The psychology of reciprocity: when someone gives you something, you feel a mild obligation to respond.
3. **Differentiating** — No other towing company in Phoenix is doing this. It immediately positions Axle Towing as the professional, relationship-focused option.

**The sequence:** Gift sent → gift received (3–4 days) → follow-up call/email (Day 5–7) → meeting → proposal → contract.

## 2. Target HOA Contacts to Send Gifts

### Tier 1: Self-Managed HOA Board Presidents

These are the highest-value targets. Self-managed HOAs have no intermediary — the board president IS the decision maker. Annual board elections mean high turnover → new decision makers with no existing vendor loyalty.

**Where to find them (from AI-2026):**

- Arizona Secretary of State entity search: https://ecorp.azcc.gov/EntitySearch/Index
- Search: Non-Profit Corporation + "homeowners association" + Maricopa County
- Look for officer names — "President" = the person to gift

**Best communities to target first:**

- Scottsdale Ranch HOA (Scottsdale — gated, 500+ units)
- DC Ranch Community Association (Scottsdale — premium)
- Ocotillo HOA communities (Chandler — large planned community)
- Ahwatukee Foothills HOA (Phoenix — 10,000+ homes)
- Fulton Ranch HOA (Chandler)
- Power Ranch Community Association (Gilbert)
- Eastmark Community Association (Mesa — one of AZ's largest master-planned communities)

**Research checklist per contact:**

- [ ] Board president name (from AZ SOS officer list)
- [ ] HOA mailing address (registered agent address)
- [ ] # of units (from Assessor data)
- [ ] Any Google reviews mentioning parking issues (proof of need)
- [ ] LinkedIn profile of president (for personalization)

## 3. Gift Sequencing: What to Send, When

### Full 3-Touch Gift + Outreach Sequence

This is the full sequence for Score 8–10 (hot) leads. Lower-score leads get abbreviated versions.

#### Touch 1: The Gift (Day 0)

**What to send:**

- Score 9–10 leads: Wireless charging pad + handwritten gift note
- Score 7–8 leads: Branded mug + mouse pad combo
- Score 5–6 leads: Branded mug only

**Gift note (print on Printify packing slip or include custom card):**

For self-managed HOAs:

> "Hi [First Name], parking enforcement is one of the trickiest parts of managing a community — unauthorized vehicles, resident complaints, liability concerns. Axle Towing specializes in private property towing for HOA communities in Phoenix. We handle enforcement professionally, 24/7, at no cost to your HOA. This is a small thank-you for the incredible work you do keeping your community running. I'll follow up this week — would love 15 minutes to show you how we work. — Elliott, Axle Towing | (480) 288-5526 | axletowing.com"

For property management companies:

> "Hi [First Name], managing a portfolio of communities means every parking enforcement headache is multiplied. Axle Towing partners with property management companies across Phoenix to handle all of it — fast, professional, 24/7. We'd love to be your preferred towing vendor across your portfolio. I'll be in touch. — Elliott, Axle Towing | (480) 288-5526"

**Printify order steps:**

1. Log in to Printify dashboard
2. Create manual order → enter recipient's office address
3. Select product(s) for that gift tier
4. In the "message" field: paste the appropriate gift note above (customized with first name and community name)
5. Process order — ship standard (3–5 days)
6. Note estimated delivery date in GHL contact record

**GHL logging:**

- Add tag: `gift-sent-[date]`
- Add note: "Gift: [product name] sent to [office address]. Est. delivery: [date]."
- Set task: Follow-up call/email on [delivery date + 2 days]

#### Touch 2: The Follow-Up (Day 5–7, ~2 days after estimated delivery)

**Channel:** Email first, then phone call same day if no reply within 4 hours.

**Email subject options (test A/B):**

- "Did the [mug/charger] make it to [Community Name]?"
- "Following up — Axle Towing for [Community Name]"
- "Quick question about parking at [Community Name]"

**Email body:**

> Hi [First Name],
>
> I sent you a little something earlier this week — hoping it made it to your desk without getting lost in the mail room!
>
> Quick intro: I'm Elliott with Axle Towing. We specialize in private property towing for HOA communities and apartment complexes throughout Phoenix. Our service is:
>
> - **24/7 dispatch** — day, nights, weekends, holidays
> - **No cost to the HOA** — we recover costs from vehicle owners at impound
> - **Fast response** — typically on-site in 30 minutes or less
> - **Full documentation** — photos, timestamps, digital logs for every tow
>
> I'd love 15 minutes to walk you through how we work and see if we'd be a good fit for [Community Name].
>
> Are you available for a quick call this week?
>
> Best,
> Elliott
> Axle Towing & Impound | (480) 288-5526 | axletowing.com

**Phone call script (if no email reply by EOD):**

> "Hi, is this [Name]? This is Elliott from Axle Towing — I sent you a [mug/charger] earlier this week, hoping to connect about parking enforcement for [Community Name]. Did you get a chance to see it? ... Great! I'd love to find 15 minutes this week — do you have time Thursday or Friday?"

**GHL logging:**

- Add tag: `outreach-active`
- Log call outcome (answered, voicemail, no answer)
- Set next task: Day 10 follow-up if no response

## 4. Personalization Strategy

### Tiered Personalization by Lead Score

| Lead Score | Personalization Level | Time Investment   | Notes                                                                       |
| ---------- | --------------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 9–10       | Full custom           | 15–20 min/contact | Custom note, community-specific research, phone call within 24h of delivery |
| 7–8        | Standard custom       | 5–10 min/contact  | Template note with first name + community name swapped in                   |
| 5–6        | Light                 | 2–3 min/contact   | Template only, no phone call, email follow-up only                          |
| 4 or below | Bulk email only       | Automated         | No gift — not cost-effective at this lead quality                           |

### What to Research Before Sending a Premium Gift

For Score 9–10 targets, spend 10–15 minutes on research to hyper-personalize:

1. **Google the community** — any recent news, new construction, HOA disputes?
2. **Check Google reviews** — are residents complaining about parking?
3. **LinkedIn search** — when did they take on this role? (New = opportunity)
4. **AZ SOS check** — when was the board president last elected?
5. **Property size** — how many units? (More units = more towing volume = bigger contract)

**Use research in gift note:**

> "I saw [Community Name] recently had [X units completed/X new phase opened] — congratulations on the growth. More residents means more parking to manage. We'd love to be part of your team."

### Property Name Personalization on Products

For Score 9–10 leads, consider ordering a mug with the community name on it (in addition to Axle Towing branding):

- Front: Axle Towing logo + tagline
- Back: "[Community Name] — Proudly served by Axle Towing"

This costs the same on Printify (just requires a different product design per order) but dramatically increases the wow factor.

## 5. Tracking Gifts Sent in GHL (GoHighLevel)

### Custom Fields to Add in GHL

Add these custom fields to the contact record (Settings → Custom Fields → Contacts):

| Field Name             | Type     | Values                                             |
| ---------------------- | -------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| `Gift Sent`            | Checkbox | Yes/No                                             |
| `Gift Item`            | Dropdown | Mug, Mouse Pad, Charger, Candle, Kit, Door Hangers |
| `Gift Sent Date`       | Date     | YYYY-MM-DD                                         |
| `Gift Delivery Status` | Dropdown | Ordered, In Transit, Delivered, Returned           |
| `Gift Response`        | Dropdown | No Response, Replied, Meeting Booked, Declined     |
| `Gift Cost`            | Number   | Dollar amount                                      |
| `Printify Order ID`    | Text     | From Printify dashboard                            |

### Smart Lists to Create in GHL

- **"Gifts In Transit"** — Gift Sent = Yes AND Gift Delivery Status = "In Transit" (follow up in 3–4 days)
- **"Awaiting Gift Response"** — Gift Delivery Status = Delivered AND Gift Response = No Response (follow up now)
- **"Gift Converted"** — Gift Sent = Yes AND Pipeline Stage = "Won" (track ROI)
- **"Gift Bounced"** — Gift Delivery Status = Returned (find correct address, retry)

### Weekly Gift Management Routine (15 minutes/week)

Every Monday:

1. Open GHL Smart List: "Awaiting Gift Response"
2. Call or email everyone whose gift delivered 3+ days ago with no response
3. Log outcomes: replied, meeting booked, no answer, etc.
4. Move converted contacts to appropriate pipeline stage
5. Move non-responders to next follow-up task

Every Thursday:

1. Identify 5 new hot leads from the AI-2026 database to send gifts next week
2. Order gifts in Printify (takes 15–20 minutes for 5 orders)
3. Tag contacts in GHL: `gift-sent-[date]`
