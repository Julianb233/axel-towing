The exact baseline was March 23, 2026:

- Organic keywords: 37
- Estimated organic traffic: 529

- Quality backlinks: about 8

The current captured state from May 14, 2026:

- Organic keywords: 66, up 29 from baseline, +78.4%
- Estimated organic traffic: 429, down 100 from baseline, -18.9%
- Authority score: 17
- Referring domains: 114
- Backlinks: 196
- SEMrush AI visibility: 30
- AI mentions: 11
- AI cited pages: 10

Client-safe framing: We have expanded Axle's search footprint and moved from no competitive AI-search citation baseline into measurable AI visibility. The next sprint has to convert that footprint into non-branded traffic, local-service rankings, owned-site citations, and better AI recommendation confidence.

## Content Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: Convert Existing Footprint Into B2B Authority

Goal: move from branded/local footprint into property-manager and AI-citation authority.

Create or strengthen:

- `/services/parking-enforcement`
- `/services/abandoned-vehicle-removal`
- `/audiences/hoa`
- `/audiences/apartment-complexes`
- `/audiences/commercial-property-managers`
- `/resources/property-manager-towing-hub`

Supporting articles:

- Arizona abandoned vehicle form guide for property managers
- Arizona towing signage requirements
- Private property towing contract guide
- How to start a private property towing program
- Arizona abandoned vehicle 72-hour rule
- Parking enforcement checklist for property managers
- HOA board guide to towing and parking enforcement
- Monthly towing compliance report template

### Phase 3: Referral Partner + Recruiting Growth Pages
