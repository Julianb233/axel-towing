---

## 1. Strategic Overview

### Why Merchandise as a Sales Tool

Cold outreach to HOA boards and property managers is noisy. Every competitor sends emails and leaves voicemails. A physical, branded gift that arrives at a decision-maker's desk is:

- **Tangible** — A mug sits on the desk every morning. A mouse pad is used 8 hours a day.
- **Reciprocal** — The psychology of reciprocity: when someone receives a gift, they feel a mild obligation to engage.
- **Differentiating** — No other towing company in Phoenix gifts HOA contacts. It instantly signals professionalism.
- **Cost-efficient** — A single HOA contract is worth $500–$2,000/month ($6,000–$24,000/yr). A $12 mug that converts 1 in 20 recipients pays back at 50x ROI.

### Dual-Purpose Store

1. **Corporate Gifting (Primary):** Branded gifts for HOA offices and property managers — the "gift before the pitch" strategy.
2. **Customer-Facing Merch (Secondary):** Public-facing store on axletowing.com for brand visibility and minor revenue.

The print-on-demand model means **zero upfront inventory cost**. Products are manufactured and shipped only when ordered.

## 2. Platform Recommendations

### Primary Platform: Printify

**Why Printify:**

- Largest supplier network — most product options
- Competitive per-unit pricing (best margins)
- Bulk order support via CSV upload for multi-recipient campaigns
- API available for website integration
- Ships directly to recipient with custom gift message

**Key specs:**

- Signup: https://printify.com/app/register
- Account type: "I want to sell online" → "My own website"
- Business name: "Axle Towing & Impound"

### Secondary Platform: Printful

**Why Printful:**

- Best quality for apparel (embroidery, DTG printing)
- Premium feel for gifting
- Slightly higher cost but better for relationship-building gifts

**Use Printful for:** Embroidered hats, polo shirts, premium apparel
**Use Printify for:** Everything else (mugs, mouse pads, keychains, accessories, door hangers)

### Alternative: Printify Free Hosted Storefront

Printify provides a free hosted storefront at `myaxletowing.printify.me` — zero development work required. Use this as an immediate Phase 1 public store while the website integration is built.

## 7. Integration with the Towing Business

### HOA Outreach Workflow

```
1. Pull hot/warm leads from GHL CRM (Score 5+)
   ↓
2. Research contact: HOA name, office address, board president name
   ↓
3. Select gift tier based on lead score
   ↓
4. Order in Printify dashboard → ship directly to HOA office address
   ↓
5. Note estimated delivery in GHL: tag "gift-sent-[date]"
   ↓
6. Day 5: Follow-up email ("Did the mug make it?")
   ↓
7. Day 5 EOD: Follow-up phone call if no reply
   ↓
8. Day 10: Value-add email (parking enforcement checklist PDF)
   ↓
9. Meeting → Proposal → Contract
```

### Locksmith & Mechanic Partner Workflow

Locksmiths and mechanics are natural referral partners — they encounter vehicles that need towing regularly.

```
1. Identify local locksmith/mechanic via referral target list
   ↓
2. Send welcome gift: keychain + air freshener + sticker
   ↓
3. Follow up: "Join our referral program — earn $25 per tow referral"
   ↓
4. After first referral: Send thank-you gift (premium multi-tool keychain)
   ↓
5. Quarterly: Send appreciation gift to active referral partners
```

### GHL CRM Tracking

Add these custom fields to GHL contact records:

| Field                | Type     | Values                                                       |
| -------------------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Gift Sent            | Checkbox | Yes/No                                                       |
| Gift Item            | Dropdown | Mug, Mouse Pad, Charger, Candle, Kit, Door Hangers, Keychain |
| Gift Sent Date       | Date     | YYYY-MM-DD                                                   |
| Gift Delivery Status | Dropdown | Ordered, In Transit, Delivered, Returned                     |
| Gift Response        | Dropdown | No Response, Replied, Meeting Booked, Declined               |
| Gift Cost            | Number   | Dollar amount                                                |
| Printify Order ID    | Text     | From Printify dashboard                                      |

**Smart Lists to create:**

- "Gifts In Transit" — follow up in 3–4 days
- "Awaiting Gift Response" — gift delivered 3+ days ago, no response
- "Gift Converted" — gift sent AND pipeline stage = Won (track ROI)

## 9. Store Setup Guide (Step-by-Step)

### Phase 1: Corporate Gifting (No Store Required) — Week 1

1. **Get logo files from Elliott:**
   - SVG vector (white on transparent + dark on transparent)
   - PNG at 300+ DPI (stacked and horizontal variants)
   - Pantone color codes for embroidery matching
   - Brand colors: Navy (#1B2A3F), Red (#DC2626), Blue (#1E6BB8)

2. **Create Printify account (30 min):**
   - https://printify.com/app/register
   - Account type: "I want to sell online" → "My own website"
   - Business name: "Axle Towing & Impound"

3. **Design first 3 products (2–3 hours):**
   - 11oz mug: logo front, "24/7 Private Property Towing | (480) 288-5526" back
   - Mouse pad: full-bleed branded design
   - Door hanger: "AUTHORIZED PARKING PERMIT" front, Axle Towing back

4. **Place test order (~$30–40):**
   - Order 1 mug + 1 mouse pad to Elliott's address
   - Review quality — logo position, print quality, color accuracy
   - Adjust if needed before bulk ordering

5. **Send first 20 gifts:**
   - Pull 20 hot leads from GHL
   - Order individually through Printify dashboard
   - Tag contacts in GHL: `gift-sent-[date]`

### Phase 2: Website Storefront — Month 2

Add `/merch` route to axletowing.com:

```
/merch              — Merch landing page + corporate gifting CTA
/merch/shop         — Customer-facing store (t-shirts, hats, stickers)
/merch/gifts        — Corporate gifting info page
```

**Options (easiest to hardest):**

- **Option A (Recommended):** Link to free Printify hosted storefront (`myaxletowing.printify.me`)
- **Option B:** Embed Shopify Buy Button ($9/month Shopify Lite plan)
- **Option C:** Printify API → custom Next.js catalog page

### Phase 3: Automated Gift Ordering — Month 3+

Integrate GHL with Printify via Zapier:

- When a lead reaches "Meeting Scheduled" stage in GHL → trigger auto-gift order
- Elliott gets SMS notification to confirm
- Gift arrives at HOA office 2–3 days before the meeting

**Required:** Zapier account + Printify integration (available in Zapier app store)
