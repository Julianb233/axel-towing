# GBP Review Response Queue

Date: 2026-05-15
Client: Axle Towing & Impound

## Strategy

Apache Junction has a large review base and low rating; Phoenix has few reviews and needs trust-building. Responding should be professional, brief, non-argumentative, and never disclose tow details publicly.

## Rules

- Do not argue about the tow in public.
- Do not cite statutes or provide legal explanations.
- Do not mention private account details, vehicle details, property details, or employee names unless already public and safe.
- Move disputes to phone.
- Mention 480-288-5526 as the resolution path.
- For positive property-manager reviews, reinforce property-manager/HOA/commercial value.

## Negative Review Templates

### Vehicle Owner Dispute

Thank you for your feedback. We understand towing situations are frustrating. For privacy and accuracy, we cannot discuss vehicle details publicly. Please call 480-288-5526 so our team can review your vehicle status and release instructions.

### Confusion About Hours

Thank you for reaching out. Towing dispatch is available 24/7, but vehicle release and office visits are handled during posted office hours or by arrangement. Please call 480-288-5526 before coming in so we can confirm current release instructions.

### Staff Complaint

Thank you for letting us know. We take service concerns seriously and would like to review what happened. Please call 480-288-5526 with the date, location, and vehicle information so our management team can look into it.

### Pricing Complaint

We understand your concern. Fees can vary based on the tow, storage, and release circumstances. For privacy and accuracy, please call 480-288-5526 so our team can review your specific vehicle record.

### Wrong Company / Not Found

We are sorry for the confusion. Please call 480-288-5526 with your license plate and tow location so we can confirm whether Axle Towing has your vehicle and direct you to the right next step.

## Positive Review Templates

### Property Manager

Thank you for trusting Axle Towing with your property. We appreciate the opportunity to support your parking enforcement and keep resident, tenant, and customer parking available.

### Fast Response

Thank you for the review. We are glad our team could respond quickly and help resolve the parking issue.

### Professional Team

Thank you for the kind words. We appreciate your feedback and will share it with the team.

## Review Request Targets

Prioritize review requests from:

- property managers
- HOA board members
- commercial property contacts
- apartment/community managers
- retail center managers

Avoid asking vehicle owners involved in disputes for reviews.
