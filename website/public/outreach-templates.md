# Axle Towing — Social Media Outreach Templates

Internal use only. These templates are for outreach to HOA board members and property managers in the Phoenix metro area.

---

## Personalization Variables

Use these placeholders in all templates. Replace before sending:

| Variable | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `[NAME]` | First name of the prospect | Maria |
| `[COMPANY]` | Company or property name | Sunrise HOA |
| `[HOA_NAME]` | HOA community name | Sunrise Ridge HOA |
| `[PAIN_POINT]` | Specific issue they've mentioned | unauthorized overnight parking |

## Target Audience Profiles

### HOA Board Presidents
- **Where to find:** LinkedIn (search "HOA board president Phoenix"), Facebook HOA groups
- **Pain points:** Resident complaints, liability concerns, unreliable towing partners
- **Key message:** Reduce board liability and resident complaints with professional enforcement.

### Property Managers
- **Where to find:** LinkedIn (search "property manager Phoenix"), Facebook PM groups
- **Pain points:** Tenant complaints, fire lane violations, time spent coordinating tows
- **Key message:** Save time and eliminate liability with a 24/7 towing partner that costs nothing.

### Apartment Complex Managers
- **Where to find:** Instagram (search apartment complex accounts), Facebook
- **Pain points:** Non-resident vehicles, no enforcement budget, resident disputes
- **Key message:** Handle parking violations fast — free service, we do the work, you take the credit.

## Best Practices

1. **Personalize every message.** Never send a template without replacing all `[VARIABLES]`.
2. **Follow before messaging.** On Instagram and Facebook, follow/friend the prospect 1–2 days before DMing.
3. **Don't over-message.** Max 3 follow-ups per prospect. If no reply after 3, mark as no_response and move on.
4. **Log every outreach.** Use the outreach dashboard at `/outreach` to track sent, replied, and converted leads.
5. **Respond quickly.** When someone replies, respond within 2 hours during business hours.
6. **Link to website.** Always include axletowing.com or a specific page link in follow-ups.
