## Arizona Parking Enforcement Law — What Chandler Property Owners Need to Know

Parking enforcement on private property in Arizona is governed by a combination of state statutes and local ordinances. Understanding these requirements is essential for any Chandler property owner considering a professional enforcement program:

**ARS 9-499.05 (Towing Signs Required):**
Private property parking enforcement requires proper signage at every entrance. Signs must be at least 18x24 inches and include the towing company name, phone number, and impound yard address. Time-limited parking enforcement requires signs that clearly state the time limit and consequences for violations.

Arizona law supports permit-based parking enforcement on private property. Sticker permits, hang-tag permits, and validation systems are all legally enforceable when backed by proper signage.
