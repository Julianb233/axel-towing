## Root Cause Analysis

### Why Axle Towing Isn't Being Cited

1. **No informational content** — AI systems cite pages that answer questions. The current axletowing.com has service pages but no blog, no guides, no FAQ that AI can pull answers from.

2. **Poor review signals** — 2.0-2.9/5 stars across platforms, 16 BBB complaints. AI systems (especially ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews) weight review scores heavily. A 2-star company will not be recommended.

3. **Thin backlink profile** — Only found in: own site, Yelp, BBB, AMA, ifindtowtruck.com, Yahoo Local, Apple Maps. Competitors have dozens of quality directories and local citations.

4. **No content differentiation** — Generic service descriptions. Nothing that makes AI "remember" this company over competitors.

5. **New website not yet indexed** — The new Next.js site (website-inky-five-49.vercel.app) is NOT appearing in any search results yet. Only axletowing.com (old WordPress site) is indexed.

6. **Zero community presence** — No Reddit mentions, no Nextdoor profile, no social media content that AI can reference.
